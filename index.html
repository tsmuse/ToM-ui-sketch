Tidies2019::Day( 01, 'Shakes' )

Solved? False

You meet your friend for coffee one day and she tells you about her brilliant new business idea: Shakespeare Shakes. She's convinced it will be the hit of the summer.

She knows that you're pretty swell with numbers, so she asks for your help: she's found a company to print Shakespeare phrases on the cups, but the problem is, they charge by the word. Luckily, they have a list of common English words (stopwords) that they'll print for free.

To stay within budget, your friend would like to only use phrases that will cost her one word. She's given you an Excel file to work with. It contains the phrases on the first sheet, and the company's list of stopwords on the second sheet. How many of the phrases she's given you fit her criteria?
